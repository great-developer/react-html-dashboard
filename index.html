Write down your Seed!

Write down your seed on a piece of paper, password manager or usb stick and store it in a safe location. It will NEVER be displayed again and is the only way to restore your wallet on any supported HD Wallet app. Your seed is stored encrypted with your password locally in your browser, it can't be restored if you forget your password.
